About Us

Our Story

Proficient Lawyer Info Tech is established by a young entrepreneurial team with a vision to help the legal fraternity to tackle the greatest obstacle that lawyers face in their profession. The goal was to create a platform that could manage an office efficiently. The lawyers are given an independent space to interact, share and coordinate the daily working of their office seamlessly.

By creating a workspace in the application you open the door to a new world of modern network management system of legal offices.

Great features of Proficient Lawyer

features that you will love

  • Case Card

    Includes Case Types, Case Numbers, Case Descriptions, Court Names, Client Details, Counsel Details and a lot more info!

  • Proceedings

    Includes an efficient management of Proceedings to help you easier.

  • Workspace Management

    Easily manage and switch between your personal & office workspace!

  • Chat and Discuss

    Chat with your colleagues with a designated chat room provided for each case for easy discussions!

  • Assign Cases

    Each case may be assigned to an associate added in a workspace with a specific message and will be notified to the assigned associate.

  • User Friendly

    Provides a lot of features designed in a way that everyone can use it at the first touch!
